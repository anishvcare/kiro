About Meridien Foods

Premium Frozen Food Specialists

Quality should never be compromised. We source premium products and deliver them through a temperature-controlled supply chain — so taste, freshness and safety are preserved from origin to your plate.

We have dedicated ourselves to providing an extensive and excellent range of products at very competitive prices, without the need to compromise on taste or quality. Our frozen range includes chicken, seafood, mutton, buffalo, duck, quail, rabbit and specialty frozen products.

Every item is carefully handled to ensure freshness, taste and safety before reaching customers. Our reluctance to compromise on quality has kept happy customers returning week in, week out.
